Support

If you need assistance with one of our products or have any questions, we're here to help. As an independent studio, we read every message and aim to provide thoughtful answers.

contact.gbostudio@gmail.com

How to reach us

When contacting support, please include as much detail as possible so we can assist you efficiently:

  • Application Name: Which product are you using? (e.g., CONSCIENCE)
  • Device Details: Your device model and OS version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 17.4).
  • Issue Description: A brief explanation of the problem or your question.

What to expect

We aim to review and respond to all support requests within 48 hours. Your feedback is invaluable to us—it helps us build better, more intentional products.
